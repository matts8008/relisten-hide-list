# Privacy Policy — Relisten Hide List

_Last updated: 2026-07-23_

**Relisten Hide List does not collect, transmit, or sell any personal data.**

- The only data the extension stores is your own settings: whether it is enabled, your list of hide words, and their on/off state.
- This data is saved using Chrome's `storage.sync` API. It stays in your browser and, if you are signed into Chrome, syncs across your own devices through your Google account. It is never sent to the developer or any third party.
- The extension runs only on `relisten.net`. It reads the currently playing track title and the track list on the page solely to decide what to skip and how to mark tracks. It does not read, store, or transmit your browsing history, credentials, or any other site data.
- There are no analytics, trackers, ads, or external network requests.

## Permissions

- `storage` — to save your hide list and settings.
- Host access to `relisten.net` — so the extension can read the player/track list and skip tracks on that site only.

## Contact

Questions about this policy can be directed to the extension's listing page on the Chrome Web Store.
